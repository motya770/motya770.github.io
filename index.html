Open Source Non-Profit AGI Simulation

Artificial Intelligence System with the Ability to Act According to Personal Interests and Needs

Based on combination of LLMs, Graph Databases and Memory

Abstract

This artificial intelligence system simulates a human persona, making decisions and taking actions based on personalized desires, goals, and needs. The system features:

  • Autonomous decision-making aligned with personal objectives
  • Memory retention of past events and experiences
  • Character simulation influencing actions and goals
  • Integration of LLMs for natural language understanding
  • Graph database implementation for complex relationship modeling
LLM Integration

Leverages advanced language models for natural interaction and understanding.

Graph Database

Stores and manages complex relationships and knowledge structures.

Memory System

Maintains and utilizes past experiences for informed decision-making.
